# Freshly Baked, Locally Loved

Welcome to The Artisan Bakery & Cafe. Located at the Sarit Centre, Westlands, we are dedicated to bringing the authentic taste of artisan baking to Nairobi. We use locally sourced, high-quality Kenyan ingredients to craft every loaf, cake, and pastry.

## Our Signature Treats
- **Artisan Sourdough:** Traditionally fermented and baked daily for that perfect crust and tang.
- **Custom Occasion Cakes:** From elegant weddings to fun birthday cakes, we bring your vision to life.
- **Flaky Pastries:** Buttery croissants, pain au chocolat, and local fruit danishes.
- **Premium Kenyan Coffee:** Expertly brewed beans from the central highlands of Kenya.

## Why The Artisan Bakery?
- **Fresh Every Morning:** We start baking before sunrise to ensure you get the freshest treats.
- **Natural Ingredients:** No artificial preservatives, just pure, wholesome ingredients.
- **Cozy Cafe Atmosphere:** The perfect spot for a quick breakfast, a business meeting, or a relaxed afternoon.

## Visit Us Today
Find us at the **Sarit Centre, Ground Floor, Westlands**. Whether you're grabbing a loaf for home or sitting in for a latte and a croissant, we're ready to serve you.
